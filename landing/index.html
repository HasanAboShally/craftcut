Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for woodworkers who want to spend less time planning and more time building.

Visual Canvas Designer

Drag, resize, and position panels in a front-view 2D canvas. See your furniture take shape in real time with snapping, alignment tools, and precise dimension inputs.

Drag & Drop • Snap Grid • Alignment

Smart Cut Optimization

Automatically arranges panels on standard sheets to minimize waste. Supports kerf width, grain direction, and edge banding — generates professional cutting diagrams.

Waste Reduction • Grain Direction • Edge Banding

3D Live Preview

Rotate, zoom, and inspect your furniture from every angle. See how panels fit together with realistic wood materials and proper Z-axis positioning.

WebGL • Orbit Controls • Realistic Materials

Print Production Booklets

Generate print-ready PDF booklets with furniture dimensions, cut lists, cutting diagrams, assembly order, and cost estimates. Take it to the workshop.

PDF Export • Assembly Guide • Cost Estimate
