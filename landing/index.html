Features

The complete workshop,
inside your browser

From your first sketch to the last screw — one tool handles design, optimization, and production planning.

Design with precision

Lay out every shelf, side, and back panel on a 2D front-view canvas. Snap guides, alignment tools, and millimeter-precise dimension editing mean you get it right the first time — before you touch a saw.

Snap Grid · Alignment Guides · Measure Tool

Minimize waste automatically

The optimizer nests every panel onto standard sheets and picks the layout with the least waste. Saw kerf, grain direction, and edge banding are all accounted for — so you buy only what you need.

Auto-nesting · Grain Direction · Kerf Allowance

See it before you build it

Orbit, zoom, and inspect a real-time 3D rendering of your assembled furniture. Panels appear with realistic wood textures in their exact positions — catch problems before they cost you a sheet of plywood.

Real-time 3D · Orbit & Zoom · Wood Textures

Step-by-step assembly guide

Get IKEA-style build instructions generated automatically. Each step shows a 3D illustration of what to attach next, which joints to use, and whether the structure needs temporary support. No guesswork at the workbench.

Auto-generated · Joint Detection · Stability Hints

Export a complete booklet

Print or PDF-export a production booklet with a cover page, dimensioned parts list, cutting diagrams with waste highlights, assembly instructions, and material costs. Hand it to your workshop or keep it at the saw.

PDF · Parts List · Cut Diagrams · Assembly

Know the cost upfront

Set the price per sheet and per meter of edge banding for your material. CraftCut totals up how many sheets you need, how much banding, and gives you a cost estimate before you drive to the lumber yard.

Sheet Cost · Edge Banding · Multi-currency

Your materials,
your rules

Each material type comes pre-configured with standard thickness, sheet sizes, and color. Need 19mm birch plywood in 2440×1220 sheets? Set it once, and every cut list and cost estimate uses your specs. You can also create fully custom materials.

  • Standard sheet sizes: 8′×4′, 8′×2′, 4′×2′
  • Per-material edge banding & pricing
  • Custom color & grain rendering
